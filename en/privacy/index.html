Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Quick Vocabnote (the "App") handles personal information.

1. Information We May Collect

The App collects only the following information.

Purchase Information

To manage the status of premium features (a one-time, non-consumable in-app purchase), the App retrieves your in-app purchase history through Apple StoreKit. This is processed via Apple's StoreKit framework and RevenueCat (a third-party service), and is used solely to verify your purchase status.

The App does not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number. We also do not collect advertising identifiers (IDFA) or usage analytics data.

2. Purposes of Use

The collected information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Enabling and verifying premium features
  • Restoring past premium purchases (Restore Purchases)

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services. Please also review each service's privacy policy:

RevenueCat

Used to manage the status of in-app purchases. RevenueCat uses only an anonymous identifier for purchase management.
https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Apple CloudKit (iCloud Sync)

Used for syncing user-created vocabulary and deck data across devices. Data is stored in the user's own private iCloud database, and the developer cannot access this data.
https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

4. Tracking and External Transmission

The App does not perform any user tracking. We do not collect advertising identifiers (IDFA), nor do we track user behavior through third-party services. The auto-translation and text-to-speech features operate entirely on-device and do not transmit any data to external servers.

5. Data Storage

User-created vocabulary and deck data are stored in the following locations:

  • A local database (SQLite) on the device
  • If the user is signed in to iCloud, the user's own private iCloud database (CloudKit)

The developer has no access to any of this data.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be changed without prior notice. The updated policy will be posted on this page.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about privacy, please contact us via our Contact form.
