Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Quick Vocabnote (the "App") handles personal information.

1. Information We May Collect

The App may collect the following information. None of this information is linked to personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

Purchase Information

To manage the status of premium features (a one-time, non-consumable in-app purchase), the App retrieves your in-app purchase history through Apple StoreKit. This is processed via Apple's StoreKit framework and RevenueCat (a third-party service), and is used solely to verify your purchase status.

Advertising Information

For ad delivery and optimization, the following information may be collected through Google AdMob (a third-party service):

  • Device identifier (IDFA) — only if you grant permission via ATT
  • Coarse location (country / region level)
  • Product interaction data within the App
  • Advertising data (ad impressions, clicks, etc.)

Diagnostics

To improve App stability and performance, the App may collect standard diagnostic data provided by Apple:

  • Crash data
  • Performance data
  • Other diagnostic data

2. Purposes of Use

The collected information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Enabling and verifying premium features
  • Restoring past premium purchases (Restore Purchases)
  • Ad delivery and optimization (Google AdMob)
  • Fraud detection and ad performance measurement
  • Improving App stability and performance

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services. Please also review each service's privacy policy:

RevenueCat

Used to manage the status of in-app purchases. RevenueCat uses only an anonymous identifier for purchase management.
https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Apple CloudKit (iCloud Sync)

Used for syncing user-created vocabulary and deck data across devices. Data is stored in the user's own private iCloud database, and the developer cannot access this data.
https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Google AdMob

Used for ad delivery and optimization. AdMob may collect information for purposes such as displaying ads, detecting invalid clicks, and measuring ad performance.
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Tracking and External Transmission

The App may track certain data (IDFA, interaction data, coarse location, etc.) through Google AdMob to optimize ad delivery.

About App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS 14.5 and later, a tracking permission dialog will appear at first launch. If you select "Ask App Not to Track", the IDFA will not be collected and only non-personalized ads will be shown.

Disabling Tracking

You can change the tracking permission for this App at any time from iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

Note that the auto-translation and text-to-speech features operate entirely on-device and do not transmit any data to external servers.

5. Data Storage

User-created vocabulary and deck data are stored in the following locations:

  • A local database (SQLite) on the device
  • If the user is signed in to iCloud, the user's own private iCloud database (CloudKit)

The developer has no access to any of this data.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be changed without prior notice. The updated policy will be posted on this page.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about privacy, please contact us via our Contact form.
